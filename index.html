Del 2 och 3 - ♥♣♦♠ A poker-like game with ES6 Classes and ES6 Modules

The hands are evaluated as follows:

  1. Highest rank in descending order:
    1. Three of a kind
    2. Two pair
    3. One pair
    4. Highest card
  2. If the ranks are the same, the sum of the threes or pairs decides the higher rank
  3. If the value is equal, it's a tie and nobody wins.
